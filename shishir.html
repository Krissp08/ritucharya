Common Health Issues + Ayurvedic Balance Tips

During Shishira, increased Vata can lead to dry skin, joint stiffness, anxiety, and constipation. To balance:

  • Keep hydrated with warm herbal teas
  • Use moisturizing oils and creams
  • Practice calming breathing exercises (Pranayama)
  • Avoid overstimulation and stress
